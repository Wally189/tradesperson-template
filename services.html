Service lines

Joinery that solves awkward spaces and improves everyday use.

Most projects fall into three areas: fitted furniture, kitchen and utility work, and repair or finishing jobs that need a reliable craftsperson rather than a rushed patch-up.

Fitted furniture

Alcoves, wardrobes, studies, and media units

Designed for rooms that need storage to feel intentional rather than bolted on. Popular commissions include shaker wardrobes, reading nooks, office joinery, and under-stair cabinetry.

Kitchens and utility rooms

Practical timberwork for hard-working spaces

Cabinet making, timber fronts, shelving, utility fit-outs, breakfast bars, and finishing details delivered either directly to homeowners or alongside designers and builders.
